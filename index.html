ABOUT

Hello, I'm Yule.
A 34 year old living it up in the big smoke of Wellington. I have two daughters, those girls are my life. Very proud mum. I've also got two cats, CATS!... why can't everything be small, cute and fluffy?

My partner and I moved here for more career opportunities. We both work in IT. The geek is strong in this house... Since the move to Wellington I have been doing contract IT work for a company in Nelson. This work consisted of mainly UI research. Previously I worked at a Nelson software development company as a front end developer for 3 years. I brought sexy back as I revamped their 1990's table based interface layout to a sleek modern interface more befitting of 2016.

I enjoy the thrill of working with and around the constraints posed by legacy web systems and the rush of creating something sleek from scratch. I've experience in mocking up, user testing and developing new user interfaces; upgrading legacy web software to be W3C standards compliant; experience with proprietary and open source libraries and documenting new system functionality.

I possess strong interpersonal skills, as well as leadership, organization, written and verbal communication, and time management skills. People say I'm friendly and bubbly, why don't you contact me to find out?
